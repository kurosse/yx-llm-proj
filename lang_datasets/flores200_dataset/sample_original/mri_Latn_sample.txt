Ko te hanami he tirotiro ki ngā pūāwai tiere, ā, kua pēnei te ahurea Hapanihi mai i te rautau tuawaru.
I ahu mai te whakaaro nei i Haina ko reira whiriwhiri ai te putiputi paramu hei kōwhiringa pua.
Ki Hapāni, ko te pua puaina tuatahi nā te emepara i whakatū mōna anake, me ōna apataki o te kāhui ariki me te kōti teitei.
I roto i te 17,000 ngā motu hei kōwhiringa, ko te kai Indonesia hei āhuatanga hamarara e whakakapi nei i te tini i ngā kai taketake ā-rohe e horapa ana i te whenua.
Ināianei kei te wātea mai ngā momo kai Javanese puta noa i te huinga motu, ā, kei a ia te whānuitanga o ngā kai he māmā noa te hā, ā, ko ngā hā matua he pai ki te hunga Javanese ko te pīnati, te hiri, te huka (inarā ko te huka kokonati Javanese), me ngā tini rau kikini whakakakara.
Mai i te 1966 kua noho iho te Sundarbans hei whakamaurutanga mohoao, ā, e ai ki te kōrero he 400 ngā tāika ariki Bengal me ngā tia korotiwhatiwha e 30,000 i roto i te wāhi.
Ko te Great Pyramid ki Giza te mōrehu kotahi anake e tū tonu ana i tēnei rā o ngā mea mīharo e whitu.
Ko Machu Picchu ngā hanganga matua e toru, arā ko Intihuatana, ko te Temple of the Sun, ko te Room of the Three Windows.
Kei te raki mā rāwhiti o Āwherika ki te Tonga te Kruger National Park (KNP), ā, kei te pae o Mozambique ki te rāwhiti, ki Zimbabwe ki te raki, ā, ko te pae tonga ko te Crocodile River.
He hirahira te pāmu renetia mō te oranga o te Sāmi, ā, ko te ahurea e hāngai ana ki te hohoko he hirahira ki ngā umanga maha.
Ko te awa Amazon te awa nunui, roroa tuarua o te ao. Neke atu i te 8 reanga wai e pūritia ana e ia.
Kei te horahanga o te 3,850 km2 ngā Sundarbans, ā, ko te kotahi-hautoru e whakakapia ana ki ngā wāhi wai/kōreporepo.
Kāore he mōhiotanga whānui hei tautohu i ngā taputapu hei taputapu nō tua whakarere. Ētahi kaiwhakahaere tāki ka tautohu i ngā taputapu nō tua whakarere mēnā e nui atu i te 100 tau te tawhito.

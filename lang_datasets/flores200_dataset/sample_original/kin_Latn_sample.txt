Kwitegereza indabyo, bizwi nka hanami, ni kimwe mu bigize umuco w'Abayapani kuva mu kinyejana cya 8.
Icyo gitekerezo cyavuye mu Bushinwa aho indabyo z’ibinyomoro byabaye ururabyo rwatoranyijwe.
Mu Buyapani, amashyaka ya mbere yindabyo za ceri yakiriwe n’umwami ku giti cye hamwe n’abandi bagize ubwami bakikije Urukiko rw’Ibwami.
Mu birwa 17,000 byo guhitamo, ibiryo byo muri Indoneziya ni uruhurirane rw’indyo nyinshi ziboneka mu gihugu cyose.
Indyo yo muri Java ibonekamo ibiryo byinshi byo mu bihe byahise, kuri ubu iboneka mu birwa byinshi, ibirungo byiganje byo muri Java harimo ubunyobwa, urusenda, isukari (cyane cyane isukari yo muri Java ikoze muri kokonati) hamwe n’ibindi birungo bitandukanye.
Kuva muri 1966 Sundarbans zabaye indiri y’inyamaswa zo mu ishyamba, kandi bivugwa ko kuri ubu muri ako karere hari ingwe z’Ubwami bwa Bengal 400 n’impongo zikabakaba 30,000.
Piramide Nini y’i Giza ni kimwe mu bitangaza birindwi bigihagaze n’ubu.
Machu Picchu igizwe n'inzego eshatu z'ingenzi, arizo Intihuatana, Urusengero rwa Temple of the Sun, Inyubako ya Room of the Three Windows.
Pariki Nkuru ya Kruger (KNP) iherereye mu majyaruguru y’iburasirazuba bw’ Afurika y’Epfo kandi iri ku mupaka wa Mozambike mu burasirazuba, Zimbabwe mu majyaruguru, naho umupaka w’amajyepfo ukaba Uruzi rw’Ingona.
Ubworozi bw’impongo bwabaye - kandi n'ubu buracyariho - ubuzima bw'ingenzi ku baturage b'Abasami kandi umuco ukikije uyu myuga ni ingenzi ku yindi myuga myinshi.
Uruzi rwa Amazone ni rwo rwa kabiri mu burebure rukaba n’urwa mbere mu bunini ku Isi. Utwara amazi akubye inshuro zirenga 8 amazi y’uruzi rwa kabiri mu bunini.

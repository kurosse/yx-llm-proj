Tu3tabar mushahadat azhar al-karaz, al-ma3rufa bi ism hanami, juz2an min al-thaqafa al-yabaniya mundhu al-qarn al-thamin.
Ja2a hadha al-mafhum min Assin, haythu kanat azhar al-khawkh hiya al-zahra al-mufadhala.
Fil Yaban, istdhafa al-embra6or 7aflat azhar al-karaz al-ula lahu faqat wa li a3dha2 akharin min al-6abiqa al-ustuqratiyya 7awla al-balat al-embra6uri.
Yu3addu atta3am al-Indonisy mustalaan shamilan yughatti majmu3a wasi3a mina al-ma2kulat al-iqlimiya al-mawjuda fi kaffat an7a2 al-bilad, wa dhalik ma3a wujud 17000 jazira lil-ikhtiyar min bayniha.
Wa yatawaffaru 7aliyan 3ala nitaq wasi3 fi jami3 an7a2 al-arkhabil, yatamayaz al-matbakh al-Jawi bi-majmu3a min al-atbaq al-mu3adda bi-basata, wa nnakahat assa2ida al-lati yufadhiluha al-Jawiyun hiya al-ful assudany wal falafil wa ssukkar (khassatan sukkar jawz al-hind al-Jawy) wa ghayriha min attawabil al-3itriya al-mukhtalifa.
Mundhu 3am 1966, kanat Sundarbans ma7miya lil-7ayah al-biriya, wa yuqaddar 2anna hunak al-2an 7awaly 400 namir binghali malaky, wa 7awaly 30,000 ghazal muraqqat fil mantiqa.
Yu3ad al haram al akbar fil Jiza a7ad al 3aja2eb assab3a al lati la tazal qa2ima 7atta al yawm.
Yatakawwan Macu Picchu min thalath bunyat sa5riyya ra2isiyyah, wa hiya intihuatana, wa ma3bad al shams, wa 7ujrat al nawafedh al thalathah.
Taqa3 7adiqat Kruger al-wataniya (Kruger National Park) fi shaـmal sharq janub ifriqya, wa hiya tamtaddu 3abra 7udud Mozambique min na7iyat asharq, wa Zimbabwe min jihat ashamal, wa ya7udduha min al-janub nahr attimsـa7.
Tu3tabar tarbiyat 7ayawan arrinna masdar rizq muhim bayna assumiyin, kama tu3ad athaqafa al-mu7ita bi-ttijara dhat ahamiya baligha aydhan lil-3adid min al-mihn al-2ukhra.
Nahr al-Amazon huwa thany atwal wa akbar nahr 3la wajh al-ardh. ya7mil akthar min 8 adh3af kammiyat alma2 allaty y7miluha thany akbar nahr.
Wa yashghal Sundarbans misa7at 3850 kilometr muraba3, tughati al-miyah/al-mustanqa3at thuluthaha taqriban.
La yujad ta3rif jami3 lil-3anasir al-masnu3a allaty tu3tabar tu7af. ta3rif ba3dh wikalat adhara2ib al-badha2i3 allaty yazid 3umruha 3an 100 3am 3ala 2annaha tu7af.
